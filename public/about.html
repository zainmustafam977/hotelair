HotelAir provides a comprehensive platform for hotel management, including:

  • Room & Booking Management
  • Guest & Staff Directory
  • Payments & Analytics
  • Calendar & Dashboard
  • Notifications & Audit Logs
  • Modern UI & Dark Mode
  • Secure Authentication
